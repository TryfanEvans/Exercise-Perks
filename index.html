What Is Exercise Physiology?

Exercise Perks is an Exercise Physiology Practice in Robe SA. Exercise Physiology aims to help individuals improve their quality of life, maintain their independence and prevent and/or manage chronic conditions/injuries through safe and individualised physical activity.

Exercise Physiologists undertake a 4 year University degree to be able to work as an Allied Health Professional. With an extensive knowledge in chronic health conditions and injuries Exercise Physiologists, are able to prescribe safe and effect exercises to those who may have cardiovascular, respiratory, metabolic (eg diabetes), neurological, mental health, musculoskeletal and other conditions.

Appropriately prescribed exercise has the potential to act as a powerful medication, helping to manage and prevent a wide range of conditions and injuries!
